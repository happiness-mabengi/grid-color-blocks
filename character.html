Tamera Campbell

Tamera is an outgoing and carefree. She's loud, fashionable, hilarious, and always creating trouble with love.

Ray Campbell

Ray is Tamera's Adoptive father. Considered to be strict, organized, and tad bit overprotective but softhearted.

Tia Landry

Tia is considered to be the responsible and more academically focused twin. She's thoughtful and always thinking ahead.

Lisa Landry

Lisa is Tia's adoptive mother. She is loud, fashionable, and hilarious.

Roger Evans

Roger is the quirky neighbor who always annoy the twins. Known for his famous line: "Go home, Roger!"
